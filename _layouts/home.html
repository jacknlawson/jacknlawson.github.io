Jack N. Lawson

I am an author, speaker and trainer working and living in East Anglia, England. On this site, you can find more information about my work and what I get up to. I am also the author of the novel 'Doing Time', a fictional account of a women's prison.

› Find out more about 'Doing Time' and buy a signed copy.

Author

'Doing Time' is my first novel, set in a women's prison in the rural American South. Annabel Lee is wrongly convicted of a crime committed by her wayward husband and gets caught up in a spiral of abuse and violence from which she can't escape.

Trainer

I offer training to numerous organisations. The type of training I offer includes, but is not limited to, Myers-Briggs Type Indicator™ and the 'Joy of Texts' series, which looks at different texts in the Hebrew Bible.

Research & Publications

I actively engage in research on various topics, including Biblical languages and the Ancient Near East.
